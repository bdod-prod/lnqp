The Challenge

Miscalculating the Demand

Businesses fail for many reasons — miscalculating product demand is one of them. Overshoot your market size and you overspend on a market where the juice isn't worth the squeeze. Undershoot and you leave opportunity on the table.

Market sizing helps you determine whether your product is a worthy investment — and how much to bet.

Our Approach

How We Size Markets

01

Foundation

  • Desk research to develop a preliminary market model
  • Integration of data on disease prevalence, reimbursement trends, prescription patterns
  • Refine assumptions based on strategic alignment and technical feasibility
02

Validation

  • Interview medical professionals and industry experts across key markets
  • Confirm demand signals, identify adoption barriers, assess practical use cases
  • Stress-test assumptions with people who know the market firsthand
03

Confirmation

  • Targeted surveys of clinicians and decision-makers
  • Evaluate adoption intent, drivers, and barriers quantitatively
  • Triangulate qualitative insights with hard data
Key Terms

Understanding Market Size

TAM
Total Addressable Market

Maximum revenue achievable at 100% market capture. The theoretical ceiling.

SAM
Serviceable Addressable Market

The portion of TAM you can realistically reach with your current capabilities, geography, and resources.

SOM
Serviceable Obtainable Market

The portion of SAM you can actually capture given your marketing, sales, and competitive position. Your realistic target.
