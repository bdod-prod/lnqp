Meet the Founder

Artyom Pugachev

Founder & Lead Consultant

Artyom is a highly entrepreneurial consultant with over 10 years of experience in growth strategy, particularly in pharmaceuticals and healthcare.

He started his consulting career at The Boston Consulting Group (BCG) in Germany, where he spent 6 years working on growth strategies, go-to-market planning, due diligences, PMI, and turnarounds.

In 2017, he founded ProteiQ - a next-generation proteomics company focused on biomarker discovery - and continued working as an independent strategy consultant on healthcare and pharma projects.

Artyom holds an MBA from London Business School and a BSc in Economics. He is based in Berlin and holds German citizenship.
