Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Data Controller

Responsible for data processing:

Artyom Pugachev
Business address: See Imprint

For any questions regarding data protection, please contact us at artyom@lnqp.io.

2. Types of Data Collected

We collect and process the following personal data:

  • Information provided via the contact form (name, company, corporate email, phone number).
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times) via Google Analytics.
  • Google Fonts usage, which may lead to a connection to Google servers.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior through Google Analytics.
  • Ensuring proper website display using Google Fonts.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contractual necessity) for handling contact inquiries.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (user consent) for Google Analytics and Google Fonts.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) for website functionality and security.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Google LLC (Google Analytics, Google Fonts).

Data transfers to the U.S. are based on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary for the intended purpose. Contact form data is deleted after responding to inquiries unless further retention is required.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete their data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting past lawful processing).
  • File a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Google Analytics uses cookies to track website usage. Users can disable cookies via their browser settings or use Google's opt-out tools.

Google Fonts may collect user data when loading fonts from Google servers.

9. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to comply with legal requirements. The latest version is always available on this website.

11. Contact

For data protection inquiries, please use the contact details in the Imprint section or email us at artyom@lnqp.io.
